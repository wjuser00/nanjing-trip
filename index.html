泡茶 · 品茶

Tea Ceremony

中国茶——不加奶、不加糖。只有茶叶、热水和耐心。祁门红茶与英国有着特殊的渊源。

Chinese tea — no milk, no sugar. Just leaves, hot water, and patience. Keemun black tea has a special connection to Britain.

中国古典音乐（古琴、琵琶、笛箫）与苏格兰传统音乐的碰撞。

关于南京

About Nanjing

🏙️ 历史

🏙️ History

中国十朝古都，2500多年历史，曾是中华民国首都。

Capital of 10 Chinese dynasties. Over 2,500 years of history. Once the capital of the Republic of China.

🌡️ 七月天气

🌡️ Weather in July

炎热潮湿——约30-36°C。上午户外活动，下午安排室内。

Hot and humid — around 30-36°C. We'll do outdoor activities in the morning or evening and save indoor ones for the afternoon.

🦆 美食

🦆 Food

南京以鸭闻名——盐水鸭、鸭血粉丝汤、烤鸭。还有小笼包和各种本地小吃。

Nanjing is famous for duck — salted duck, duck blood vermicelli, roast duck. Also soup dumplings and local snacks.

🍵 茶文化

🍵 Tea Culture

中国发明了茶。我们不加奶不加糖——和英国茶完全相反。Wesley Jay 会展示真正的喝法。

China invented tea. We drink it without milk or sugar — maybe different from British tea. We can try it together.
